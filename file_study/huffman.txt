The Water-Sprinkling Festival is the New Year celebration for the Dai people, typically observed on the 15th of April each year. It takes place across the Dai autonomous prefectures and counties in Yunnan. Usually, the festival starts on April 12th and continues until April 17th, lasting for five days. During this time, the entire city shuts down, and everyone participates in the festivities.
